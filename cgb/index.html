Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions clients ask about website
development, timelines, and support.

How long will it take to build my website? +
A basic website can take 1–2 weeks. More complex sites may take several weeks based on features and revisions.
Will my website work on all devices? +
Yes, all websites we develop are responsive, ensuring they work perfectly on mobile, tablet, and desktop.
Can I make edits to my website myself? +
We offer CMS options or custom admin panels so you can update text, images, and more without coding.
What is the cost of a website? +
Prices vary based on features. After understanding your goals, we provide a fixed quote with no hidden fees.
Do you provide post-launch support? +
Yes! We offer maintenance plans for updates, backups, and technical support after your site is live.
